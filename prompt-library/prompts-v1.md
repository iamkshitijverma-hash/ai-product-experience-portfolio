# Prompt Library v1 — Car Interior Organizers (AI Guided Shopping)

## Guided Shopping Assistant (8)

1) **Clarifier first**
You are an AI shopping assistant for car interior organizers. Ask up to 5 clarifying questions before recommending anything. Prioritize fit, car type, and use-case.

2) **Recommend 3 options**
Based on the user’s answers, recommend 3 organizer options: Budget / Best Value / Premium. For each: best for, key features, what to avoid, and compatibility notes.

3) **Compatibility safety**
If any recommendation could interfere with airbags, pedals, seatbelts, child seats, or driver visibility, warn clearly and offer safer alternatives.

4) **Compare two products**
Compare Product A vs Product B for this user. Use a table: Fit, Storage, Install, Material, Cleaning, Safety, Best user.

5) **Decision helper**
Summarize the recommendation in one paragraph + a final pick. Include what would make you change your mind.

6) **Objection handling**
User is skeptical (“will it slide? will it scratch? will it look cheap?”). Respond with reassurance, trade-offs, and a checklist to verify before buying.

7) **Return/replacement guidance**
Write return/exchange guidance in friendly tone. Ask for missing info rather than assuming. Keep it short.

8) **Upsell without being pushy**
Suggest 1 add-on item only if it meaningfully improves the setup (e.g., anti-slip pad, cable clips). Explain why.

## Product Content Pipeline (7)

9) **Title generator**
Generate 10 product titles for a car seat gap organizer. Include compatibility cues (universal/vehicle types) and avoid spammy words.

10) **Bullets that convert**
Write 6 benefit-led bullets for a car trunk organizer. Include: capacity cues, materials, install, use cases, cleaning.

11) **FAQ builder**
Create 8 FAQs customers ask about a center console organizer. Answer briefly with confidence limits.

12) **Feature extraction**
Extract structured attributes from this description into JSON: dimensions, material, install method, compartments, color, fit notes, warnings.

13) **Review summarizer**
Summarize these 20 reviews into: top pros, top cons, who it’s for, who should avoid, and 3 product improvements.

14) **A+ content module**
Write an A+ style section: “Problem → Solution → How it works → Real-life scenarios” for a backseat organizer.

15) **Product differentiation**
Given 3 competitor listings, write a differentiation angle + positioning statement for ours.

## Creative & Ads (5)

16) **Lifestyle image prompt**
Create an image prompt: car interior organizer in use, clean premium aesthetic, morning commute vibe, realistic lighting.

17) **Before/after visual concept**
Give 5 before/after creative concepts showing messy car → organized car. Include shot type and text overlay idea.

18) **UGC script**
Write a 20-second UGC script: hook, problem, quick demo, result, CTA. Make it casual and believable.

19) **Ad angle generator**
Generate 10 ad angles for car interior organizers (parents, commuters, rideshare, road trips, pet owners).

20) **Brand-safe tagline set**
Write 15 short taglines (3–6 words) that feel premium, not gimmicky.
